By Aneliese Moeller

The ocean is my favorite place to relax and forget about the stressful things, with its endless blue waves sparkling in the sunlight. Ever since I was a baby I've always loved the ocean, I was never scared of it. I especially love sunrise and sunset, the sky reflects stunning colors on the water, turning it into a canvas of orange, pink, and gold. Beneath the surface, colorful fish and coral create a vibrant underwater world. I love snorkling and go almost every year. This past year I went snorkling with my friend and we saw so many creatures.
